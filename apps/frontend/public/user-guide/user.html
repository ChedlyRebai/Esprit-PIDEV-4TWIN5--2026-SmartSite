👤 User Role Guide

Welcome to SmartSite! As a User, you have access to essential platform features that will help you contribute effectively to projects and team collaboration.

🎯 Your Core Responsibilities

📊 Dashboard Access

  • View your personalized dashboard with key information
  • Monitor project updates and announcements
  • Track your assigned tasks and deadlines
  • Access important notifications

📋 Task Management

  • View and manage your assigned tasks
  • Update task status and progress
  • Add comments and attachments to tasks
  • Mark tasks as complete when finished

👤 Profile Management

  • Update your personal information
  • Manage your contact details
  • Set your preferences and notifications
  • Upload profile picture

📈 Reporting

  • View basic project reports
  • Track your personal performance metrics
  • Generate simple progress reports
  • Export data for external use

🏗️ Project Participation

  • View project progress and timelines
  • Access project documents and files
  • Participate in project discussions
  • Submit timesheets and work logs

💬 Team Communication

  • Communicate with team members
  • Participate in team discussions
  • Share updates and progress reports
  • Collaborate on project documents

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Complete Your Profile: Start by updating your personal information in the Profile section
  2. Explore Dashboard: Familiarize yourself with your dashboard layout and features
  3. Check Tasks: Review your assigned tasks and understand their requirements
  4. Join Projects: Explore projects you're involved with and understand your role

📅 Daily Workflow

Morning Routine

  • 📊 Check your dashboard for updates and new tasks
  • 📋 Review your task list for the day
  • 📧 Check for any important notifications

During the Day

  • ✅ Work on your assigned tasks and update progress
  • 💬 Communicate with your team as needed
  • 📊 Track your time and submit updates

End of Day

  • ✅ Update task completion status
  • 📝 Submit any required reports or timesheets
  • 📋 Plan for tomorrow's priorities

💡 Pro Tips

  • 📱 Stay Updated: Check your dashboard regularly for new tasks and updates
  • 💬 Communicate: Keep your team informed about your progress and any issues
  • 📊 Track Time: Accurately track your time for better project planning
  • 📚 Learn: Take advantage of training resources to improve your skills
